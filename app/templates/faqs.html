Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

VlogX Admin is the management dashboard for the VlogX platform. Here, you can manage drivers, customers, vehicles, orders, payments, and view analytics for your logistics business.

Go to the Driver Management section, review the driver's details and documents, then use the toggle to approve or reject the driver.

Use the Mailing and Messaging section in the side menu. You can compose and send broadcast emails to all customers or all drivers from there.

The dashboard and driver detail pages provide comprehensive analytics on trips, ratings, and earnings. You can also export reports if needed.

Go to the relevant user (driver or customer) detail page and use the password reset option if available, or contact support for assistance.

Please contact the Blueducks Limited Software Company support team at support@blueducksltd.com for any technical issues or questions.
